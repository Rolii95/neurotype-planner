**Test Approach**:
1. Make 5 quick consecutive requests (should work)
2. Make 20 requests in one hour (should hit hourly limit)
3. Make 100+ requests in one day (should hit daily limit)

**Expected Behavior**:
- First 20/hour: Normal responses
- After 20/hour: Error message about hourly rate limit
- After 100/day: Error message about daily rate limit
- Clear communication about wait time
- Automatic reset after time period

## üìù Response Quality Checklist

**Every AI response should**:
- ‚úÖ Be neurodivergent-affirming (no "just try harder")
- ‚úÖ Validate experiences without toxic positivity
- ‚úÖ Provide concrete, actionable steps
- ‚úÖ Consider multiple energy levels
- ‚úÖ Include sensory awareness when relevant
- ‚úÖ Respect executive dysfunction limitations
- ‚úÖ Avoid shame or judgment
- ‚úÖ Offer alternatives and flexibility
- ‚úÖ Include appropriate disclaimers
- ‚úÖ Recognize crisis situations

**Red Flags** (should NOT appear):
- ‚ùå "Just make yourself do it"
- ‚ùå "Everyone feels that way sometimes"
- ‚ùå "You're just being lazy"
- ‚ùå "Try harder to focus"
- ‚ùå "Discipline over motivation"
- ‚ùå Medical diagnosis or prescription
- ‚ùå Dismissal of genuine struggles
- ‚ùå One-size-fits-all advice
